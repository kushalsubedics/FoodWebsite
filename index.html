Signature Dishes

  • Momo
    • Momos are delicious Nepalese dumplings filled with spiced meat or vegetables, steamed to perfection. These bite-sized delights are often served with tangy and spicy dipping sauces. Their tender, juicy filling and thin, delicate dough make them a favorite appetizer or snack, offering a burst of flavor in every bite.
  • Chicken Curry
    • Chicken curry is a rich and flavorful dish made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a fragrant blend of spices, tomatoes, onions, and garlic. The curry boasts a thick, aromatic sauce that perfectly complements the succulent chicken, creating a comforting and satisfying meal enjoyed with rice or bread.
  • Chowmein
    • Chow mein is a savory Chinese stir-fried noodle dish featuring tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and flavorful sauces. The noodles are lightly browned and tossed with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil, creating a deliciously aromatic and satisfying meal. It's a perfect harmony of textures and tastes in every bite.
